# Project Phases

## Phase 1 — MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

The MVP focuses on delivering a fully functional e-commerce platform with the minimum set of features required to validate the business model.

Included:

- User Authentication
- Customer Account
- Product Catalog
- Product Search
- Shopping Cart
- Checkout
- Shipping
- Payment
- Order Management
- Notifications
- Admin Panel
- CMS
- Website Builder

Excluded:

- Voucher
- Coupon
- Flash Sale
- Wishlist
- Product Recommendation
- Loyalty Program
- Promotion Engine

---

## Phase 2 — Growth

Growth focuses on improving customer engagement and increasing conversion.

Included:

- Voucher
- Coupon
- Flash Sale
- Wishlist
- Product Recommendation
- Promotion Engine
- Customer Reviews
- Product Rating
- Analytics Dashboard

---

## Phase 3 — Enterprise

Enterprise focuses on scalability and advanced business capabilities.

Included:

- Multi Warehouse
- Multi Vendor
- Multi Store
- AI Recommendation
- AI Website Builder
- Business Intelligence
- ERP Integration
- CRM Integration
